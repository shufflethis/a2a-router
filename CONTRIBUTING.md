# Contributing to a2a-router

Thank you for your interest. This project is in **public RFC + bridge MVP phase**. The public A2A/MCP bridge endpoints are live, and the Trust-Pledge standard still needs serious review.

## What we need

### 🔍 RFC feedback
The most valuable contribution right now is reading and commenting on:
- [RFC-001: Trust-Pledge Specification](docs/rfc/RFC-001-trust-pledge.md)
- [Technical Architecture](docs/architecture.md)
- [Competitive Analysis](docs/competitive-analysis.md)

**How:** Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/shufflethis/a2a-router/discussions) with specific section/line references.

### 🛡️ AI safety review
The Trust-Pledge test suite needs review from people who do red-team work.
- Are the 5 commitments comprehensive?
- Are the audit categories the right ones?
- What's missing from the test methodology?

### 🏢 Enterprise procurement perspective
If you work in enterprise procurement or compliance:
- What trust signals would actually move a buying decision?
- How would Trust-Pledge interact with SOC 2 / ISO 27001?
- What's the right audit price point?

### 🌍 Internationalization
Especially welcome:
- German translation (we're a German project)
- Portuguese (large emerging market)
- Japanese (active agent ecosystem)

### 🔗 Cross-registry conversations
If you maintain a registry (Smithery, Glama, MCPize, etc.) or know someone who does:
- We want Trust-Pledge to work across registries
- Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/shufflethis/a2a-router/discussions) or reach out by [X DM](https://x.com/wuebbe). No public email is published.

### 💸 Contributions
If you want to support the project financially, use [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/a2amcp). This is separate from protocol trust, audit status, and any future routing or listing decisions.

## What we don't need yet

- ❌ PRs that require credential persistence, private-network routing, or settlement before those designs are reviewed
- ❌ Claims that planned registry or settlement features are already live
- ❌ Paid placement disguised as Trust-Pledge verification

## How to contribute

### Discussions (preferred)
1. Browse open [Discussions](https://github.com/shufflethis/a2a-router/discussions)
2. Comment on existing ones or open a new one
3. Be specific — quote the section you're responding to

### Issues
Issues should be:
- Specific to a document/spec
- Actionable
- Single-topic

### Pull Requests
For now, PRs are welcome for:
- Documentation fixes (typos, broken links)
- Translation additions
- Diagram improvements

For substantive content changes, **open a Discussion first**.

## Code of conduct

Be the kind of person you'd want to debug a distributed system with at 3am. Specifically:
- Disagree without being unkind
- Critique ideas, not people
- Quote what you're responding to so people can follow
- Assume good faith
